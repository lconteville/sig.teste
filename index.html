The SIG: IN-SILICO Talks: DRUG-DESIGN comprises a Special Interest Group (SIG) organized by members of the RSG-Brazil, a well-established student network recognized as a member of the International Society of Computational Biology Student Council (ISCB-SC). This one-day focused meeting aims to promote scientific endeavor and professional exchange in the computational biology approaches applied to developments of personalized medicine, pharmacogenomics, exome/genome analysis, and drug-design. The event is planned to be free and virtual.

------- CALL FOR PAPERS IS OPEN

We welcome abstracts from all levels of research students and early career post-doctoral researchers. The abstracts must fall within the event topics.
